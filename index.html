Expert Learning Tips

The R-G-C Formula

Role: Tell AI who it is (e.g., "Act as a historian").
Goal: Define the task.
Context: Provide background details.

Iterative Feedback

Never settle for the first answer. Ask the AI to "Refine," "Shorten," or "Add more examples."
